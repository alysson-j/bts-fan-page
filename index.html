Welcome to BTS!

BTS (Bangtan Sonyeondan) is a South Korean group known for their music, choreography, and worldwide influence. This site introduces their members, music, achievements, and fanbase. BTS offically debuted on June 13, 2013 under Big Hit Entertainment. Their hits mix pop, hip-hop, and heartfelt messages about growing up, mental health, and self-love.
