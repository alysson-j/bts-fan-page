The BTS ARMY

ARMY is the official fandom of BTS—standing for Adorable Representative M.C for Youth. BTS established their fandom name on July 9, 2013

What ARMY Does

  • Supports BTS online and at concerts
  • Organizes charity events
  • Promotes positive messaging
